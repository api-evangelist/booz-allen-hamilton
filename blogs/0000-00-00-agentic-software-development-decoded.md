This Velocity Magazine article explores how AI agents can automate nearly every phase of the software development lifecycle to help federal agencies accelerate delivery and modernize legacy systems. It presents a human-centric framework showing how agentic AI shifts development from "AI coding" to "AI-enhanced delivery" across requirements, design, build, testing, and operations. Published in Velocity Magazine V4, Fall 2025.
