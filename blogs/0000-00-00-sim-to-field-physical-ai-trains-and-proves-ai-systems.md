Physical AI integrates high-fidelity simulations with real-world data to enable reliable training and validation of autonomous systems. The article describes using "digital proving grounds" to test AI behavior across thousands of scenarios before deployment, reducing the sim-to-real gap and building auditable evidence of safety and readiness for mission-critical applications. Published in Velocity Magazine V4, Fall 2025.
