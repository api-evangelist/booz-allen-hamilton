The article examines how silicon photonics (SiPh) uses light instead of electrons to overcome data-transfer limitations in modern computing, promising gains in bandwidth, latency, and energy efficiency. It notes that "SiPh goes beyond Moore's Law to break the efficiency wall that limits all-electronic devices" and frames the technology as a focus of U.S.-China geopolitical competition. Published in Velocity Magazine V4, Fall 2025.
